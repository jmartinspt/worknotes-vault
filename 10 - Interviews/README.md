# Interviews

Interview notes, candidate evaluations, and hiring process documentation. Structure for both conducting interviews and tracking interview experiences.

## Purpose

This folder contains:
- **Interview preparation** materials and questions
- **Candidate evaluation** notes and assessments
- **Interview feedback** and recommendations
- **Hiring process** documentation and workflows
- **Personal interview** experiences and learnings
- **Follow-up actions** and next steps

## Types of Interviews

- **Technical interviews** - Coding and system design
- **Behavioral interviews** - Culture fit and soft skills
- **Management interviews** - Leadership and team dynamics
- **Informational interviews** - Learning about roles or companies

## Structure

Interview notes should include:
- **Interview details** (date, time, position, interviewer)
- **Preparation notes** and research
- **Questions asked** and answers provided
- **Candidate assessment** (if conducting)
- **Key insights** and impressions
- **Follow-up actions** and next steps
- **Decision rationale** and recommendations

## Template Usage

Use templates from `_templates/interviews/` for consistent interview documentation and evaluation processes.

## Organization

Interviews can be organized by:
- **Interview type** - Technical, behavioral, etc.
- **Position or role** - Different job categories
- **Company or team** - Different hiring managers
- **Interview stage** - Screening, technical, final

## Integration

Interview notes should link to:
- **Contacts** involved in the interview process
- **Projects** or roles being discussed
- **Tasks** for interview preparation or follow-up
