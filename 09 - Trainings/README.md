# Trainings

Training materials, learning paths, and skill development tracking. Document educational content, course notes, and professional development activities.

## Purpose

This folder contains:
- **Training course notes** and materials
- **Learning path documentation** and progress tracking
- **Skill development plans** and objectives
- **Workshop notes** and key takeaways
- **Certification preparation** materials
- **Conference notes** and insights

## Structure

Training content should include:
- **Course objectives** and learning goals
- **Key concepts** and important information
- **Practical exercises** and hands-on activities
- **Resources and references** for further learning
- **Progress tracking** and completion status
- **Application notes** - how to apply learnings

## Organization

Training can be organized by:
- **Subject area** - Technical skills, soft skills, domain knowledge
- **Training type** - Courses, workshops, conferences
- **Skill level** - Beginner, intermediate, advanced
- **Provider** - Different training organizations or platforms

## Template Usage

Use the training template (`_templates/training/`) for consistent documentation of learning activities and progress tracking.

## Integration

Training notes should link to:
- **Projects** where skills will be applied
- **Tasks** for practicing new skills
- **Contacts** who are mentors or subject matter experts
